ONE PIECE

  • INTRODUCTION

    • One Piece is an ongoing anime featuring 1,118 episodes. Its manga counterpart, which has 1,126 chapters and is still ongoing, was first serialized in Weekly Shōnen Jump in 1997. The manga is created by Eiichiro Oda. The anime, produced by Toei Animation, has been airing since 1999. One Piece has garnered a massive global fanbase and holds the Guinness World Record for the best-selling manga series of all time. It has one of the largest and most dedicated fan bases in the anime and manga community.


  • STORY

    • The story of One Piece stands out from other anime by focusing on both the protagonist and the side characters, each with unique backgrounds and motivations. The protagonist, Monkey D. Luffy, aspires to become the Pirate King. As of now, Luffy has a crew of 10 members, each with their own dreams and personalities. Luffy was inspired by Red-Haired Shanks, a pirate captain who visited his hometown, showing him the true spirit of adventure. Luffy accidentally ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi, a Devil Fruit that gave him rubber-like powers.
